About

I hold an M.Sc. in Range Management (Socio-Ecology) from the University of Nairobi, specializing in ecosystem dynamics and sustainable land management. With extensive field experience across 15+ countries in Africa and Asia, I bring deep cross-cultural understanding and practical insights into environmental conservation. Fluent in English, French, Swahili, and Kikuyu, I effectively collaborate with diverse stakeholders across international development contexts, combining academic rigor with on-the-ground expertise in climate action and carbon management.
